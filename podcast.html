Bangladesh, COVID-19 & Climate Change

on April 26, 2020

We know coronavirus attacks our respiratory system. Dhaka has consistently ranked number 1 as the city with the most polluted air. Are we ready to fight COVID-19 with our already damaged lunge breathing polluted air? To what extent, Bangladesh is responsible for this level of pollution.

বাংলাদেশ: করোনভাইরাস এবং জলবায়ু পরিবর্তনের মধ্যে প্রত্যক্ষ সম্পর্ক
